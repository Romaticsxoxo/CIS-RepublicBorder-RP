Our Divisions

While every soldier begins their service in the Droid Army as a B1 or B2 unit, dedication and skill are rewarded. Prove your worth to gain access to our elite divisions, each with specialized roles, equipment, and prestige. You can also serve as a Death Watch warrior by acquiring the gamepass. All roles, standard or elite, participate in raids, game nights, and coordinated training sessions.

Commando Droids

Core Function: Premier infiltration and strike units. Engineered for stealth and tactical superiority, they excel in covert operations, assassinations, and supporting fleet actions.

Perks & Benefits:

  • Active command structure with dedicated Officers and HICOM.
  • A professional and welcoming community.
  • Regularly hosted divisional events.
  • Access to new, custom morphs.
  • Specialized loadout including a Vibrosword.

How to Join:

Entry is selective. Candidates must pass a tryout hosted periodically on our Discord server. Stay active and watch for announcements.

MagnaGuards

Core Function: The elite protectors of the CIS leadership. Armed with fearsome electrostaffs, they are the unyielding line of defense for High Command members and key Separatist Council VIPs.

Perks & Benefits:

  • Active and engaged Officer/HICOM leadership.
  • A challenging but supportive community focused on excellence.
  • Unique events centered on protection details.
  • Access to some of the best droid morphs in the game.
  • Specialized loadout featuring the bullet-deflecting electrostaff.

How to Join:

Entry is selective. Candidates must pass a tryout hosted periodically on our Discord server. Stay active and watch for announcements.

Codex & Handbook

Rules of Conduct

  1. Respectful Conduct: All members must be respected. Toxicity, arguments, harassment, and racism will not be tolerated.
  2. Language: English is the only language permitted in the server to ensure effective moderation.
  3. Appropriate Content: No NSFW, inappropriate topics, or obscene jokes are allowed.
  4. No Advertising: Do not advertise other groups or services in the server or via DMs to our members.
  5. No Exploits: Do not attempt to find and abuse loopholes in the rules. Report any discovered exploits to High Command.
  6. No Impersonation: Impersonating CIS or TGR command members is strictly prohibited.
  7. No Alternate Accounts: Members are limited to one account in the server.

Chain of Command

  • Report High Command (C1-C3): Contact General Kalani (C4).
